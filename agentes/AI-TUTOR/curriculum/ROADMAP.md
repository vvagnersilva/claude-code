## How to use this roadmap
- `/learn` always proposes the next unmastered topic of the current stage.
- `/onboarding` runs a **placement diagnostic**: any topic you demonstrably already
  know is marked ✅ and skipped, but its flashcards are still scheduled to confirm
  retention.
- Stages **spiral**: later stages deepen and reuse earlier ones. Connect new ideas
  back to mastered ones.

## Mastery rubric (applied per topic)
1. **Recall** — can define it with no undefined sub-terms.
2. **Apply** — can use it correctly, unaided.
3. **Predict / Produce** — can anticipate the result or produce the artifact.
4. **Transfer** — can choose it appropriately vs. alternatives and explain why.

A topic is ✅ **mastered** only at level 4 (transfer).
